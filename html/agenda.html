This video is a statement against the idea of the gay agenda which was born from negative beliefs from the LGBT community. It is supposed to be an unkind expression towards gay people but many of us have turned it into a joke, to make fun of those who believe that the spread of homosexuality is a bad thing. The reason why this is turned into a joke is because it seems to be the way of coping these days. In an age of memes combined with the same homophobic culture that sent kids out their homes or to their deaths so long ago, we cope with jokes.
