Sometimes, the noise won't turn off.

This work was inspired by what I myself experience.

Anxiety, depression, the day's events--sometimes things flash through your mind, one stack of thoughts after another, seemingly endless. Calm seems futile. In a never ending buzz of thoughts, there is no rest.
